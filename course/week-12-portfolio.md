# Week 12: Evidence and Technical Communication

## Goal

Make the work reproducible and honest for an independent reviewer.

## Final package

- architecture and threat model
- dataset card and annotation guide
- benchmark with grouped and out-of-distribution splits
- baseline-to-cascade comparison
- calibration and threshold memo
- error and robustness analysis
- service and monitoring evidence
- model card and production-readiness checklist
- five-minute demo and fifteen-minute deep dive

## Review questions

- What is the simplest adequate method?
- Which score changed your design decision?
- Which failure remains dangerous?
- What evidence would cause rollback?
- What did you deliberately not build?

## Deliverable

A repository that a reviewer can clone, test, and understand without private context or unverifiable claims.
